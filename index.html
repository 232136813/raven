Focus on your writing

Raven is a minimalistic, distraction-free text editor for novelists, with beautiful typography and organisational features.

Download the beta

More than meets the eye

All your novels in one place

Raven's library keeps track of all the novels you have ever worked on, and provides quick access to every one of them. Being centralised, it also works well with cloud storage such as Dropbox or OneDrive, just select a folder that's synced and you're good to go!

Noise-Free Writing Experience

We've took out all of the unnecessary features so that you can focus only on getting those words down.

Keep Everything Together

Raven's organisational features allow you to keep everything related to your novel in one place, and provides quick access to all of them. (Currently only the novel outline is implemented)

Let The World See It

Raven allows exporting your novel to a range of formats. (Currently only Markdown and WebPage export formats are supported)

Got Questions? Problems? Suggestions?

Raven is an open-source software, and contributions of any kind are always welcome. If you think you've found a problem with Raven, or think it should do something it doesn't, use the issue tracker and we'll get on to it.

Raven's community aims to be inclusive and safe for everyone. Please read the code of conduct. For any questions and concerns, you may contact me by email or Twitter.
